# Product Requirement Document
## Farm Company Equipment CRM Dashboard

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** March 28, 2026  
**Target Platform:** Responsive Web Application  
**Document Owner:** Product Management

---

## 1. Executive Summary

The Farm Company Equipment CRM Dashboard is a responsive web application designed to empower farmers with intuitive management of their agricultural equipment and service operations. The application provides a centralized platform for equipment tracking, service request management, resource access, and user profile management. This PRD outlines all functional and design requirements for the initial release.

---

## 2. Product Vision & Goals

### 2.1 Vision Statement
To simplify farm equipment management and service operations, enabling farmers to make informed decisions about their machinery with minimal friction.

### 2.2 Primary Goals
- **Ease of Use:** Enable farmers of varying technical backgrounds to navigate and manage equipment effortlessly
- **Operational Efficiency:** Reduce time spent tracking equipment status and service requests
- **Information Access:** Provide immediate access to critical equipment data and support resources
- **Engagement:** Maintain farmer engagement through clear communication and intuitive design
- **Accessibility:** Ensure the platform works reliably in low-connectivity rural environments

### 2.3 Success Metrics
- User adoption rate among registered farmers
- Average session duration and feature utilization rates
- Service request submission rate and completion time
- User satisfaction scores (NPS)
- Support ticket volume reduction

---

## 3. Target Users

### 3.1 Primary Users
- **Farmer Profiles:** Farm owners and operators managing 1-500+ acres
- **Technical Proficiency:** Low to medium; many may have limited smartphone experience
- **Device Usage:** Primarily Android devices; secondary iOS support
- **Network Conditions:** Variable connectivity; must support offline-first design patterns

### 3.2 Secondary Users
- **Farm Managers:** Employees managing equipment on behalf of farm owners
- **Service Technicians:** Field technicians accessing service request details
- **Support Staff:** Internal support team resolving farmer inquiries

### 3.3 User Pain Points
- Difficulty tracking multiple equipment units and maintenance schedules
- Time-consuming service request submission processes
- Lack of centralized access to equipment documentation
- Fragmented communication across service interactions
- Uncertainty about equipment status and upcoming maintenance
